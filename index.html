About Me

Hi! I'm Jessica, a Stockholm-based author specializing in romantasy, where love and magic intertwine in richly imagined worlds. With a passion for storytelling that blends epic fantasy with heart-stirring romance, I craft tales filled with powerful heroines, enchanting realms, and slow-burn relationships that linger long after the final page.

When I'm not writing, I can be found exploring cozy bookstores, sipping tea in hidden cafés, or getting lost in the pages of my next favorite novel.

My debut novel, The Lunar Oath, is the first in a sweeping romantasy series that promises adventure, intrigue, and an unforgettable love story.

Books

The Lunar Oath

A stolen artifact. A kingdom on the brink of darkness. A reluctant alliance between a runaway princess and a rogue thief. As they race against time to restore balance, forbidden feelings threaten to unravel everything. But some oaths demand more than they ever expected to give.

The Ember Throne

Peace was never meant to last. With war on the horizon and a hidden power awakening, the battle for the throne is only beginning. Magic, betrayal, and destiny collide as an exiled heir must decide what they are willing to sacrifice—for the crown, for the kingdom, and for love.
